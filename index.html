ABOUT ME

Born and raised in South Africa, Mayur Diar is a multi-skilled UX/UI & Graphic Designer, based in
Atlanta, GA. When Mayur runs into bad UX he can’t help but do something about it, often going
above and beyond to redesign the entire experience.

Mayur has a unique ability to shape his designs into compelling visuals that help to support the
narrative he’s trying to communicate.

He has extensive design experience in the greater Atlanta area and has focused on continuously
improving himself through various degrees, including a certificate in UX/UI Design from Georgia
Institute of Technology, an MBA from Keller Graduate School of Management and a BFA in
Graphic Design from The Art Institute of Atlanta.
